About Safar

Safar is dedicated to taking the stress out of roadtripping. We love the journey, but hate worrying about how to get to the destination.

Our team is made up of 4 students at the University of Notre Dame who have a passion for travel and software development. Safar is the result of a project for CSE 40793.
