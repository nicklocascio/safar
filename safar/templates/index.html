About

Safar

Safar is a road trip companion service that makes your road trips better. Through our technology, you get to plan your trip around the United States over any number of days you'd like. You'll also get suggestions for places to stay, eat, see, and so much more. Get ready to make your road trips seamless.

Our values

Making road trips easier...

Easy

Instead of getting lost between different websites trying to make your road trip great, Safar does all of the work for you. Just sit back, pack your bags, and leave the rest to us.

Multi-Function

Our technology helps you navigate between cities and states while also making your road trip experience that much more insightful and enriching. Prior to the trip, Safar helps you get all the stops you need so you don’t have to worry about getting lost, tired or confused.

Efficient

If you don't want to plan a trip and would just like to explore, there are multiple custom trips you can choose from that people have tried and recommended before.

Colorado

Colorado has a diverse landscape of arid desert, river canyons and snow-covered Rocky Mountains, which are partly protected by Rocky Mountain National Park. Elsewhere, Mesa Verde National Park features Ancestral Puebloan cliff dwellings. Perched a mile above sea level, Denver, Colorado’s capital and largest city, features a vibrant downtown area.
